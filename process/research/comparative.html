Comparative Analysis


I am aiming to make a project out of creating awareness of water pollution through interactive web experiences catered to kids. This project by Lien AID made a similar concept of creating a mobile game to represent water pollution in its simpler form. It has similar ideas to my concept by using examples such as avoiding the dumping of trash and plastic materials into the ocean with the result of consequences of either notifications or deduction of points stating a message that it is showing a negative impact into the ocean. My concept differs and adds to this example by adding sea creatures in the game. In the game, as trash and plastic particles are dropped in the ocean, the result would be unhappy sea creatures underneath. This is showing the visual effects of the consequence of water pollution that is appealing to a wide audience specifically to kids. Creating a positive awareness and sharing an important message for change.

This is a website called spent which is an interactive installation game that shows awareness by providing an experience for the user to play the spending game. This game shows cases on how people that are unemployed struggle to make ends meet. It also highlights laws that make their struggles a lot worse than it seems. It is similar to my concept in a way it shows an interactive experience for the user at the same time teaching them of the current issues that are happening in today’s time in terms of pollution. The difference of my concept to this example is the theme of my project. I will be focusing on the issue of water pollution while this website has a broad description of unemployment issues in the US.
